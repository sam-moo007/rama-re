# Product

## Register

product

## Platform

web

## Users

Dubai property investors, home buyers, and property owners seeking data-backed confidence, historical DLD transaction verification, interactive financial scenario modeling, and 3D/XR virtual tours.

## Product Purpose

RAMA is a verified Dubai real estate decision platform that eliminates market asymmetry by pairing real-time Dubai Land Department (DLD) data, AI advisory, cost scenario modeling, and interactive 3D spatial experiences.

## Positioning

The data-verified decision platform for Dubai real estate investments.

## Brand Personality

Nordic clarity, architectural precision, calm confidence, transparent.

## Anti-references

SaaS cliché dark modes, neon purple/pink gradients, noisy real-estate portal listing badges, fake countdown timers, aggressive popups, over-rounded 32px+ card cards.

## Design Principles

- Verified over visually hyped: Every price, DLD transaction, and risk indicator must display with clean, authoritative precision.
- Nordic spatial clarity: Warm canvas neutrals, restrained shadows, tactile 44px+ control targets, and intentional layout rhythm.
- Decision room focus: Minimize cognitive noise so complex multi-scenario property comparisons remain readable.
- Seamless multi-modal depth: Effortless transitions between spatial 3D maps, historical comparables, document vaults, and AI advisory chats.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Target WCAG 2.1 AA compliance with minimum 4.5:1 contrast ratio for body text.
- Standardized touch target heights (44px min touch/click areas).
- Reduced motion support on all animated spatial components.
