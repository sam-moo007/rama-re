# Support Briefing: Nordic Companion Pivot

**Confidential — Internal Support Teams Only**

## Overview
Starting in Q3, we are rolling out a major update to the RAMA platform. We are pivoting away from complex gamification (like the "TrustPassport") and technical terminology toward a "calm, plain-language" decision companion. 

## FAQ for Users

### "Where did my TrustPassport go?"
- **Response**: "We've upgraded our verification system to be more transparent. Instead of a 'TrustPassport' score, you'll now see exactly how many key facts we've verified (for example, '18 of 22 key facts verified'). This makes it much easier to understand exactly what information is confirmed and what is still pending."

### "Why can't I see the evidence coverage percentage anymore?"
- **Response**: "We found that percentages were sometimes confusing—what does an 85% coverage actually mean for your purchase? We now use plain sentences to tell you exactly what documents or facts are verified, so you know exactly where you stand."

### "Why do I need to sign in to save a property now?"
- **Response**: "You can still browse and compare properties as a guest! However, to ensure your saved properties are securely synced across all your devices (phone, tablet, computer), we now ask you to create a quick account when saving your first home."

### "Where is the Decision Room?"
- **Response**: "We've simplified our navigation. What was previously called the 'Decision Room' is now simply the 'Property details' page, where you'll find all the verification and cost information you need."

## Escalation Path
- **Technical Bugs (e.g., missing verification data)**: Escalate to `#eng-nordic-pivot` on Slack.
- **Legal Questions about "Verified" claims**: Escalate to the Legal Team.
- **Negative User Sentiment**: Log in the Product feedback board and tag the Product Manager.
