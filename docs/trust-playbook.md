# RAMA Trust Playbook & Terminology Glossary

## 1. Operating Model & Boundaries
- **Licensed Brokerage:** RAMA operates strictly within the bounds of a licensed brokerage/referral service under DLD regulations.
- **Data Verifiability:** Every data point MUST be verifiable via an official source (e.g., DLD, RERA, Trakheesi).

## 2. Content-Risk Tiers
- **Tier 1 (High Risk):** Title deeds, legal ownership, escrow accounts. (Source of truth: DLD).
- **Tier 2 (Medium Risk):** Floor plans, built-up areas, amenities. (Source of truth: Developer declarations).
- **Tier 3 (Low Risk):** Aesthetics, views, subjective neighborhood descriptors. (Source of truth: Verified agents).
